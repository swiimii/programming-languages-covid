COVID-19 Information

Mission Statement:

We aim to increase knowledge and awareness of COVID-19 to better combat the virus. Currently, the best way to reduce the spread and flatten the curve is through everyone's cooperation and working together to do so. Through ignorance and misinformation, some people choose to disregard best practices. It is our hope that by providing an easy means of consuming COVID information, a way to confirm learning (and validate Facts over Myths), and a means of social pressure via score comparison, we can spur more people to action to stop the spread.
